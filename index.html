Protect the Hive!

You're a general in the bee army of your hive. Your hive has come under attack by a group of wasps!

Your division of warrior bees stands ready to defend the queen. Below you have your four best soldiers. Each one deals a specific amount of damage.

Click on your soldiers to trigger their attack on the invading wasp. Be careful! Wasps are cunning and strong warriors and can only be defeated by dealing a precise amount of damage. This will be displayed as health specific to each attacking enemy.

Use different combinations of strikes to deal the exact amount of damage needed to take out a given wasp. Too much damage will attract the attention of nearby wasps who will rally to the rescue of their fallen comrade and surely wipe out your division.

If you defeat a wasp, move on to the next with a win in your corner. Otherwise, it will count as a wasp. Each enemy will have a different level of health and the effectiveness of your warriors attacks will vary between enemies.

Good luck! Fight hard, defend the Queen, and protect the Hive!
